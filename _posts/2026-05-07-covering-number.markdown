Let $1\leq p < q \leq \infty$. For all $\epsilon\in(0,1)$ and $d\in\NN$,

$$
\log N(B_p,\|\cdot\|_q,\epsilon)\asymp_{p,q} \left\{\begin{matrix}
 d\log\frac{e}{\epsilon^s d}  & \epsilon \leq d^{-\frac{1}{s}} \\
 \frac{1}{\epsilon ^s}\log(e\epsilon ^s d)  & \epsilon \geq d^{-\frac{1}{s}}
\end{matrix}\right.,\quad \frac{1}{s}\overset{\text{def}}{=}\frac{1}{p}-\frac{1}{q} .
$$
