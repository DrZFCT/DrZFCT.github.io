*We investigate how well common LLM alignment methods preserve the inherent diversity of human preferences. The three major contributions are as follows. First, we quantify the expressivity of reward-based methods. Second, we prove that the optimal solution to [NLHF](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v235/munos24a.html) is a mixed strategy if and only if a pairwise winner exists within the preference relationship, along with providing other fine-grained characterizations. Third, under the Luce model, we establish the non-asymptotic rate of the above event.*
